## Detailed Descriptions

### 1. User Identity & Session
- **AuthUser**: Represents the currently logged-in user in the frontend application.
- **UserDoc**: The root document structure stored in the `users` collection in Firestore.

### 2. Health & Lifestyle
- **Lifestyle**: Captured during onboarding. Tracks basic habits like smoking, drinking, and chronic conditions.
- **UserProfile**: A more detailed profile stored in the `users/{uid}/user_health_details/main` document. Includes metrics like BMI (weight/height), age, and family history.

### 3. Activity Logs
- **SleepLog**: Daily records of sleep duration and quality. Stored in `users/{uid}/sleepLogs`.
- **HabitLog**: Daily records of habits (water, exercise, stress, etc.). Stored in `users/{uid}/habitLogs`.

### 4. Prediction Engine
- **DiseaseRisk**: Not a stored entity, but a structure returned by the `analyzePredictions` function. It evaluates risk for:
    - Cardiovascular Disease
    - Type 2 Diabetes
    - Chronic Sleep Disorder
    - Anxiety & Depression
    - Hypertension
    - Respiratory / Lung Disease
