## Detailed Use Case Descriptions

### 1. Manage Authentication
- **Description:** The user creates an account, logs securely into the app, or ends their session.
- **Includes/Extends:** Registration, Secure Login via, Logout flows.

### 2. Manage User Profile
- **Description:** The user provides their baseline health metrics. This data acts as the baseline for algorithms.
- **Includes/Extends:** Updating age, gender, height, weight, prior existing medical conditions, and family health history.

### 3. Log Daily Habits & Exercise
- **Description:** The user inputs details regarding their physical activities, water intake, stress levels, and meal counts.
- **Related To:** Directly influences the Dashboard summary and AI predictions algorithms.

### 4. Log Sleep Duration & Quality
- **Description:** The user specifies their bedtime and wake time. The system calculates duration and takes into account perceived sleep quality.
- **Related To:** Essential for establishing baseline health stats.

### 5. View Daily Dashboard
- **Description:** Allows the user to quickly glance at today’s progress.
- **Includes/Extends:** Displays aggregated daily stats generated by Habit & Sleep inputs.

### 6. Access AI Risk Predictions
- **Description:** The system analyzes the user's logged data (requires at least 3 days of habits and sleep data) and user profile to predict the likelihood of major diseases (Cardiovascular, Diabetes, Anxiety, etc.).
- **Includes/Extends:** Displays Improvement Tips and Contributing Factors for each analyzed disease.

### 7. Analyze Health Statistics
- **Description:** Provides historical charts and metrics comparing data over days, weeks, and distinct months.
- **Includes/Extends:** Time-series filters and averages.

### 8. Delete Erroneous Logs
- **Description:** Users can securely remove a past sleep or habit log if they inputted it incorrectly.
